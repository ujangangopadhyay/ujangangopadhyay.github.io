About me

I am a postdoctoral research fellow at the Department of Mathematics at the National University of Singapore. My mentor is Prof. Subhro Ghosh. In August 2021 I completed PhD in Mathematics from the University of Southern California. My advisor was Prof. Ken Alexander. Prior to that, I received Bachelor of Statistics and Master of Statistics degrees from the Indian Statistical Institute. My research area is Probabilty, Mathematical Physics, Statistics, Machine Learning. With Prof. Ghosh I co-organize the Stochastics Seminar at the National University of Singapore.
